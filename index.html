Synopsis

In the rain-slick streets of a city that never sleeps, a retired radio host receives a midnight transmission that promises to reveal the truth behind a decades-old disappearance. As the voice on the other end of the line guides him through cryptic clues, he begins to suspect that the signal is connected to his own forgotten past.

The Midnight Signal is a slow-burning psychological thriller that explores memory, guilt, and the dangerous power of stories. The film blends noir aesthetics with modern technology to craft a voyeuristic tale of obsession and redemption.
